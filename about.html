ABOUT US

P Stothard & Associates provide consulting and technical services for the integration of advanced technologies into industry. We develop custom instrumentation and data acquisition systems and provide electrical and electronic installation services.

Our custom data acquisition systems are based on computer based instrumentation technologies. We also develop computer games based virtual reality mine site simulations and visualisations.

Our focus is system design, human computer interfaces, spatial awareness training and hazardous site environment visualisation. All solutions are tailored to the needs of the client and systems can be used for environmental monitoring and compliance or system control.

P Stothard & Associates develop solutions for mining, construction, environmental impact assessments, geophysics and heavy industry.

Services offered include:

  • 3D Modelling and Animation
  • Consulting and Technical Services
  • Environmental Visualisation
  • Feasibility Studies (Technology)
  • Fieldwork
  • Project Management
  • Research and Development
  • Training Course Design
  • Virtual Instrumentation

P Stothard & Associates is based in the South West of Sydney NSW.
