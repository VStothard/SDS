ABOUT US

Simulations Design Services provides consulting and technical services for the integration of advanced technologies into industry. We develop custom instrumentation, data acquisition systems, and installation services.

Our custom data acquisition systems are designed around computer instrumentation technologies and allow us to develop virtual mine site simulations and visualisations, presented in a computer game format.

Our focus is system design, human computer interfaces, spatial awareness training, and hazardous site environment visualisation. All solutions are tailored to the needs of the client and systems can be used for environmental monitoring and compliance or system control.

Simulation Design Services develop solutions for mining, construction, environmental impact assessments, geophysics and heavy industry.

Services offered include:

  • 3D Modelling and Animation
  • Consulting and Technical Services
  • Environmental Visualisation
  • Feasibility Studies (Technology)
  • Fieldwork
  • Project Management
  • Research and Development
  • Training Course Design
  • Virtual Instrumentation

Simulation Design Services is based in the South West of Sydney NSW.

Contact us to discuss your simulation, visualisation, training simulator or data acquisition needs.
