Applications to Industry

Virtual reality simulations have proven extremely valuable in cases where the work environment is difficult to access or creates issues with logistics and safety. This is especially the case in industries such as mining, where training in real life situations is imperative to the success of organisational operations, but where there is a high risk of injury or death if mistakes are made in a real life training situation.

Simulations can be used to develop safe work procedures, safety management plans and risk assessments through collaborative teams, and the presentation of complex data in an intuitive graphical format easily comprehended by experts and non experts alike. Simulations allow for mistakes to be made in a safe and forgiving environment, ensuring the safety of all employees while introducing them to the risk and hazards of their work environment.
